The Treeist

Pruning

We provide a variety of pruning methods

  • Crown Pruning
  • removes dead and defective branches
  • Structrual Pruning
  • corrects problematic growth, such as codominant stems and over-extended limbs
  • Raising
  • increases clearance for structures and access.
  • Thinning
  • increases sunlight penetration and air movement
  • Pollarding
  • this is for highly constrained growth and flower production

We use spikeless climbing techniques and try to make cuts that are few, small, and fast healing. More generally, we respect the gudelines of ISA's Best Management Practices: Tree Purnning(2008) and the associated ANSI standards.
